Welcome to Bukit Jelutong

Bukit Jelutong is an upscale suburb of Shah Alam, Selangor, Malaysia. The suburb is a planned community, with an area of over 2,200 acres and developed by Guthrie Property Holding Berhad

About Us

The Bukit Jelutong community has proven to be a close community judging from its regular events. The main yearly event is the Bukit Jelutong Carnival held annually. The Carnival is organised by the Bukit Jelutong Residents Association. Currently, most of the areas in Bukit Jelutong are gated and guarded through supports from the community itself.

Masjid Tengku Ampuan Jemaah, located in Bukit Jelutong, Shah Alam, Malaysia, stands as a majestic symbol of Islamic architecture and spiritual sanctuary. Named in honor of the late Tengku Ampuan Jemaah, the royal matriarch of Selangor, this mosque is renowned for its exquisite design and cultural significance.

Navigating Bukit Jelutong is made easy with Rapid KL's SA05 bus line, stopping at key locations like Space U8 Shopping Mall, Balai Bomba & Penyelamat Kota Anggerik, and Radia Bukit Jelutong Sales Gallery. The closest station, D'Bayu Commercial Centre, is a short 14-minute walk away. Additional buses run at 5:46 PM and 6:06 PM. Perfect for commuters, the route covers essential spots such as schools and Plaza Jelutong, ensuring convenient access to various destinations in the area.
